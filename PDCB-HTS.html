High-Throughput Sequencing Analysis with Bioconductor

This was a semester course I taught to students from the PhD in Biomedical Sciences program at the Center for Genomic Sciences (UNAM). I had a lot of fun teaching this course as most of the students were already my friends and everyone was highly motivated to learn more. It sure was peculiar to teach a PhD level course when I had not enrolled in a PhD program myself yet! First of all, please check the syllabus for the course before proceeding. Note that a basic knowledge of R was a prerequisite before joining the course. Below you will find links for most of the material.
